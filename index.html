Controls:

  • Click + Drag the cube to adjust the viewing angle
  • ⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️: Change cube selection on a layer
  • Shift + ⬆️⬇️: Move to a new layer
  • Numbers: Change the input of the selected cube

Rules:

  • Just like Sudoku, fill the cube with numbers 1 through N so that each row, column, and layer has no repeats.
  • Just like KenKen, each cage's cubes (highlighted in color) must equal the displayed result. E.g. 5+ means that all 3 colored cubes must add to 5. 1- means that the 2 cubes must subtract to equal 1.
